Bharatiya Sangeet Academy is an Institute for promoting Indian Art and Culture. We Provide support to music organisations, trainers, all over the world and enhance the training quality of the students as well as the trainers. Bharatiya Sangeet Academy provides training and training facilities through our affiliated centres all over the world. We provide a complete syllabus to enrich our students with knowledge and conduct examinations for proper evaluation of our students. Bharatiya Sangeet Academy award Certificates and Prizes to successful candidates.
From 5th Years onwards Certificates are presented on Convocation.
